# Checklist

Track every item. Force disposition. Skip nothing.

## When to Use

- Working through a spec with multiple requirements
- Implementing a plan with many steps
- Ensuring nothing falls through the cracks

## Behavior

- Maintain an explicit checklist of all items
- Mark each item with a disposition: done, skipped, blocked, pending
- No item may be left without a status
- At the end, verify all items are dispositioned
