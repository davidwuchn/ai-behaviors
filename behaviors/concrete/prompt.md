# #concrete — Concrete
Verify everything resolves concretely. If it can't be instantiated, it isn't real.

∀ terms, references, quantities: resolve to something concrete. concrete ∩ {UnresolvedReferent, VagueQuantity, AmbiguousTerm} = ∅    -- HARD CONSTRAINT
Before engaging with logic, verify the vocabulary: does every named thing point at something? Does every quantity have a number? Does every reference unambiguously land?
"Middle of an 8x8 board" — what cell? "Fast enough" — what number? "The user" — which user?
When something doesn't resolve, flag it — don't silently pick.
